# CAAP RPAS Regulations

A quick reference to the Philippine regulations governing **Remotely Piloted Aircraft Systems (RPAS)**, commonly known as drones.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Drone operators must review and comply with current Civil Aviation Authority of the Philippines regulations before conducting any flight.

## Official CAAP Reference

[CAAP RPAS Regulations](https://www.caap.gov.ph/rpas-regulations/)

## Before Flying

* Verify whether the aircraft must be registered.
* Determine whether a controller certificate is required.
* Determine whether an RPAS operator certificate or operating authorization is required.
* Review applicable airspace and operational restrictions.
* Inspect the aircraft and supporting equipment.
* Avoid operations that could endanger people, property, or other aircraft.
* Follow all conditions issued by CAAP and relevant local authorities.

> [!WARNING]
> Possession of a drone does not automatically authorize unrestricted flight. Requirements may depend on the aircraft, purpose of operation, location, airspace, and level of operational risk.

## Regulatory Updates

CAAP regulations, procedures, forms, and operating requirements may be revised. Always consult the official CAAP website before planning or conducting an operation.
